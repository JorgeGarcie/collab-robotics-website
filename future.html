Future Steps

As we look ahead, we plan to further enhance our system with the following improvements:

  • Lightweight Image Processing: We intend to explore alternatives to YOLO. While YOLO is effective, its heavy computational requirements make it less ideal for real-time performance. A lighter model would allow for faster, more efficient image processing.
  • Expanding Object Recognition: Our current system is focused on detecting fruits. Moving forward, we aim to broaden our detection capabilities to include a wider range of objects, making the system more versatile.
  • Enhanced Grasping Algorithms: We plan to implement more advanced grasping techniques that can generalize across various objects. This will enable our robot to handle different tasks with greater precision and adaptability.
